Mahabharata in 10 Lines

  1. The Mahabharata is an ancient Indian epic attributed to the sage Vyasa.
  2. It narrates the Kurukshetra War between the Pandavas and Kauravas.
  3. Composed of around 100,000 verses, it's one of the longest epic poems.
  4. Themes include dharma, karma, and the nature of existence.
  5. Characters like Yudhishthira, Bhima, Arjuna, Duryodhana are central.
  6. It contains moral dilemmas, philosophical discourses, and intricate subplots.
  7. The Bhagavad Gita, a sacred scripture, is part of the Mahabharata.
  8. It explores themes of love, betrayal, loyalty, and honor.
  9. The epic has profound influence on Indian culture, philosophy, and religion.
  10. Continues to be celebrated worldwide for its timeless wisdom and universal themes.
